Welcome to Newfoundland Sikh Temple

Nfld Gurudwara Sahib was established by a handful of families in 2005 with the aim to contribute towards the community and promote the teachings of Sikhism.

Sikhism is a monotheistic religion founded in the Punjab region of India in the 15th century by Guru Nanak Dev Ji. It emphasizes equality, selfless service, and devotion to God.

About Us

Nfld Gurudwara Sahib was established by a handful of families in 2005 with the aim to contribute towards the community and promote the teachings of Sikhism.

We are dedicated to serving the community and promoting the teachings of Sikhism. Our temple offers a welcoming environment for all to learn, worship, and grow together.

Services

We offer daily prayers, community services, and educational programs for all ages. Our goal is to foster a sense of unity and spiritual growth within the community.

  • Daily prayers and worship
  • Community kitchen (Langar)
  • Educational programs and classes
  • Community outreach and support

Events

Join us for our upcoming events and celebrations. Stay tuned for more details about our festivals, community gatherings, and special ceremonies.
